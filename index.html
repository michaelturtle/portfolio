About me

I'm Michael Turtle, an Australian journalist who left his job in television to travel the world forever. My travel blog, Time Travel Turtle, is a collection of my stories about this journey. I share the world with my followers through my writing, photography, videos and podcasts. I also work with brands and destinations on digital marketing campaigns to share their key messages.
